Automobile Case Study
Business problem

A car manufacturer wants to reduce unexpected vehicle breakdowns.

The organization collects the following data:

Feature	Example value
Mileage	72,000 km
Months since last service	14 months
Engine alerts	5
Brake wear	82%
Battery voltage	Low
Customer complaints	3
Step 1: Descriptive Analytics

The company analyzes previous vehicle-service records.

Findings
30% of vehicles with mileage above 70,000 km experienced component failures.
Vehicles with brake wear above 75% had more emergency service requests.
Vehicles not serviced for over 12 months had twice the failure rate.
Battery-related complaints increased by 15% during winter.
Question answered

What happened in the past?

Step 2: Predictive Analytics

A machine-learning model processes the current vehicle information.

Model output
Probability of service failure in the next 30 days: 89%
Risk category: High Risk
Question answered

What is likely to happen?

Step 3: Prescriptive Analytics

Based on the prediction, the system recommends:

Contact the customer immediately.
Schedule a service appointment within 48 hours.
Inspect the braking system and battery.
Reserve required spare parts.
Provide a temporary replacement vehicle, when required.
Question answered

What should the company do?

Comparison
Parameter	Descriptive	Predictive	Prescriptive
Main question	What happened?	What is likely to happen?	What should be done?
Time focus	Past	Future	Future action
Purpose	Understand performance	Forecast outcomes	Recommend decisions
Input	Historical data	Historical and current data	Predictions, rules, costs and constraints
Output	Reports and dashboards	Forecasts and probabilities	Recommended actions
Complexity	Low to medium	Medium to high	High
Example	200 vehicles failed last month	40 vehicles may fail next month	Service these 40 vehicles first
